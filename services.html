Expert Legal Services

We provide a full spectrum of legal services tailored to protect your rights, your family, and your business interests.

Civil Litigation

Our civil litigation team provides robust representation in all civil disputes. Whether you are pursuing a claim or defending one, we bring strategic thinking, thorough preparation, and courtroom excellence to every matter.

  • Contract disputes and breach of contract claims
  • Debt recovery and collection proceedings
  • Damages claims (personal injury, defamation, negligence)
  • Urgent interdicts and interdictory relief
  • Appeals and reviews in the High Court and Supreme Court
  • Alternative dispute resolution and mediation
Discuss Your Case

Family Law

Family matters require both legal expertise and emotional sensitivity. Our family law team handles every case with compassion, discretion, and an unwavering commitment to achieving the best possible outcome for you and your loved ones.

  • Divorce proceedings (contested and uncontested)
  • Child custody, access, and guardianship disputes
  • Child and spousal maintenance applications
  • Antenuptial contracts and cohabitation agreements
  • Adoption and surrogacy matters
  • Domestic violence protection orders
Get Family Law Advice

Commercial Law

From startups to established enterprises, we provide comprehensive commercial legal services that help your business thrive. Our commercial team combines legal acumen with business insight to deliver practical, results-driven solutions.

  • Company registration and business structuring
  • Drafting and review of commercial contracts
  • Mergers, acquisitions, and due diligence
  • Corporate governance and compliance advisory
  • Shareholder agreements and disputes
  • Labour law and employment contracts
Protect Your Business

Property Law

Property transactions are significant investments that require careful legal oversight. Our property law team ensures your real estate dealings are secure, compliant, and in your best interest from start to finish.

  • Property transfers and conveyancing
  • Bond registrations and cancellations
  • Lease agreements (residential and commercial)
  • Sectional title disputes and body corporate matters
  • Property litigation and eviction proceedings
  • Township establishment and land use applications
Secure Your Property

Criminal Law

When your freedom is at stake, you need a defence team that fights relentlessly. Our criminal law attorneys have extensive experience representing clients at every level of the South African criminal justice system.

  • Bail applications (police station and court)
  • Trial representation in Magistrate's and High Court
  • Appeals and reviews of convictions and sentences
  • Fraud, theft, and corruption matters
  • DUI and traffic-related offences
  • Domestic violence and assault cases
Get Criminal Defence

Estate Planning & Wills

Planning for the future is one of the most important decisions you will make. Our estate planning team ensures your legacy is protected, your wishes are honoured, and your loved ones are provided for.

  • Drafting of wills and testamentary trusts
  • Deceased estate administration and reporting
  • Trust creation and administration
  • Estate duty planning and tax efficiency
  • Curatorship and guardianship applications
  • Estate litigation and will disputes
Plan Your Estate
